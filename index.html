How is this different from asking ChatGPT?
ChatGPT doesn't know your ROAS, your active channels, your competitors, or the specific frustration you described in the intake form. The audit is built from your answers — every grade references something you told us. The AI handles the analytical framework. The judgment — what actually matters for your specific brand — is the part that requires real expertise.
Why is it delivered in Notion, not a PDF?
A PDF is a document you read once and close. The Notion workspace includes your channel scorecard, a 90-day plan as an actual checklist you can tick off, and a comment thread for follow-up questions over 30 days. It's designed to be the marketing operating system you work from — not a report that gets filed.
Do I have to commit to a retainer?
No. The $299 audit is a standalone product. Most clients end up in a monthly engagement because the audit surfaces enough work to justify it — but that conversation happens after you've seen the thinking. Not before.
What verticals do you work in?
Shopify DTC brands doing $200K–$2M in annual revenue. That's the only audience this audit is built for. The intake form, the grading rubrics, the competitor framework, and the 90-day plan format are all designed specifically for DTC operators at this stage.
How long does the intake form take?
About 10 minutes. The most important question is Q7 — your biggest frustration — and the quality of your answer there directly determines the quality of your diagnosis. The more specific you are, the more specific your root-cause analysis will be.
