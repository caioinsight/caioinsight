## Products + Pricing

### Entry — AI-Ready Audit ($399)
- 10-minute Tally intake form
- Store scanned for AI visibility signals
- 7-section Notion workspace in 48 hours
- No discovery calls. Async by design.

### 7 Report Sections
1. Brand snapshot
2. Channel scorecard (A–F per channel)
3. AI Visibility Score (0–100) — schema markup, llms.txt, review velocity, LLM citations, GEO content
4. Root-cause diagnosis
5. 3 quick wins (30-day, revenue impact estimated)
6. Competitor gap analysis
7. 90-day dual plan

### Core Retainer — $2,000/month (no contract)
Monthly AI visibility report, one GEO initiative/month, channel tracking,
unlimited async Q&A, quarterly re-audit. Cancel anytime.

### Add-ons
- Strategy call: $99 (30-min audit walkthrough)
- AI Readiness Toolkit: $197 (self-serve GEO framework)
- White-label license: $2,000–$5,000 one-time + $297/month
- Newsletter paid tier: $9/month (The Owned Channel)

## ICP
**Primary:** Shopify DTC brands
- Revenue: $200K–$2M annual
- Stage: 12–36 months, founder-run
- Ad spend: $2K–$15K/month
- Pain signal: "What do I do now that Meta doesn't work like it used to?"

**Secondary verticals:**
- Franchise systems (multi-location, compliance-aware GEO)
- Cannabis CPG — reference ONLY as "compliance-constrained verticals" publicly

## Positioning
- Only fractional CMO showing thinking before retainer commitment
- Only audit in $200K–$2M DTC segment grading channel performance AND AI visibility
- Trust-reversal model: $399 audit first, retainer optional

**The AI angle:**
- Shopify reported 11x increase in AI-attributed orders in 2025
- 85–92% of Shopify stores invisible to AI shopping agents
- No competitor serves $200K–$2M DTC segment with GEO intelligence
- Window: 12–18 months before enterprise tools go downmarket
