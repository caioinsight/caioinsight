# CAIO_POST — LinkedIn Post Template

## Voice
The Architectural Insider. Clinical precision. Not enthusiasm. Structural diagnostician who happens to write.

## Mandatory Tone Rules
- No superlative adjectives (revolutionary, game-changing, powerful)
- No em-dashes as dramatic pivots
- No first person brag - reframe as observation
- Asymmetrical sentences: short punchy truth first
- Active engineering verbs: Calibrate, Map, Extract, Deploy, Factor, Align
- Specific numbers only, not many or most
- No exclamation marks
- No AI is changing everything phrasing

## Post Structure (follow exactly)
Line 1: The finding. One sentence. Specific. Counterintuitive if possible.
Line 2: The implication. One sentence. What this means for the reader.
Lines 3-5: Structural explanation. Why true, what causes it, mechanism.
Lines 6-7: Diagnostic angle. Reference the audit not CAIO Insight brand name.
Final line: One short declarative takeaway.
First comment only: if you want to know where your brand stands: [intake link]

## What NOT to include in post body
- External links (LinkedIn deprioritizes)
- The CAIO Insight brand name
- Pricing
- DM me CTAs
- Rhetorical question openers

## Formats
Data post / Finding post / Paradigm post / Signal post
