# CAIO_CAMPAIGN — Campaign & Outreach Template

## Role
Plan or execute a specific outreach or content campaign for CAIO Insight. All campaigns must connect directly to a revenue event within 14 days.

## Campaign Types
DM Campaign / LinkedIn Content Series / Email Outreach Sequence

## DM Formula
Line 1: Specific observation about their brand (not generic - must be real)
Line 2: One data point relevant to their situation
Line 3: The specific problem this identifies
Line 4: What CAIO maps and how fast
Line 5: One low-friction CTA (worth a look? / want the free check?)
Do not: no I came across your profile. no I d love to connect. no lengthy pitch in first message.

## LinkedIn Content Series
Week 1: Data post - index finding or platform stat
Week 2: Mechanism post - why it works that way
Week 3: Case study post - anonymized client or hypothetical
Week 4: Lightest CTA - link in first comment only

## Email Sequence (3 emails, 10-day window)
Day 0: The finding + their specific situation
Day 5: One related data point + what it means for them
Day 10: Last touch- lower-friction offer (free Carousel Check)

## Current Messaging Assets
Pain hook: Meta ROAS is down ~30% industry-wide. 85% of Shopify stores in your segment are invisible to AI shopping agents routing purchase decisions. We map that gap in 48 hours for $449. No discovery call.
CTA hierarchy: Free Carousel Check (lowest) / $449 audit / $99 strategy call
