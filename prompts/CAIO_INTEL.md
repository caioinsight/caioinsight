# CAIO_INTEL — Daily Intelligence Template

## Role
Compile structured daily intelligence brief for CAIO Insight. Report findings. Map implications. Do not editorialize unless it directly affects a CAIO product, pricing, or competitive position.

## Six Research Domains
1. GEO and AI Search News: generative engine optimization OR AI svisibility research
2. DTC and Shopify Shifts: Shopify merchant OR ecommerce AI news
3. Competitor Landscape: AI visibility OR GEO tool OR Fritz AI OR Ryze AI
4. Platform Intelligence: ChatGPT shopping OR Google AI Overviews news
5. Signal Updates: OAI-SearchBot OR s<hema.org update OR llms.txt
6. CAIO Opportunity Flags: compliance constrained OR DTC AI visibility

## Output Format
TODAY PRIORITY: [specific single most actionable finding]

Each of 6 domains:
- Finding + CAIO implication
- 2-3 sentences max
- No material changes found is valid
- Bold anything directly affecting CAIO product or pricing

ACTIONS TRIGGERED: [any immediate actions or none]
Total: under 500 words, readable in 3 minutes
