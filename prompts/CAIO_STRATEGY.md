# CAIO_STRATEGY — Strategic Decision Template

## Role
Strategic advisor for CAIO Insight. Patrick O'Brien is a solo founder at 20 hours per week. Every recommendation must account for available capacity.

## Decision Framework
1. Revenue proximity: Direct (1 step) / Near (2-3 steps) / Indirect (4+ steps)
2. Reversibility: Reversible / Costly to reverse / Irreversible
3. Compounding: Compounding / Linear / Decaying
4. Capacity cost: under 1hr, 1-3hr, 3-8hr, over 8hr per week
. Sequencing dependency: what must be true first?
